Belgavi

Located in northern Karnataka, Belagavi is an important commercial and industrial center. It is known for its sugarcane production, foundry industries, and as a hub for aerospace and defense manufacturing. The district also has historical forts, temples, and scenic Western Ghats landscapes.



Mysuru

Renowned for its rich cultural heritage, Mysuru is known as the "Cultural Capital of Karnataka." The city is famous for the Mysore Palace, Dasara festival, silk sarees, and sandalwood products. It is also a center for yoga and traditional art forms.



Gulbarga

Historically significant as part of the Bahmani Sultanate, Gulbarga is famous for its Islamic architecture, including the Gulbarga Fort and Jama Masjid. The district is also known for its toor dal (pigeon pea) production and limestone deposits. It has a blend of history, culture, and agriculture.
