  • Mahavatar Narasimha




    • Mahavatar Narsimha is a recently released animated feature film based on the story of Narasimha and Prahlada. The film has been noted for its visual grandeur and imaginative world-building, blending two major episodes from the Dashavatara (ten avatars) of Vishnu. It explores themes of divine justice, unwavering faith, and the eternal promise of divine protection. The film has been successful at the Indian box office, becoming the first Indian animated film to enter the 200 crore club, according to Koimoi.
